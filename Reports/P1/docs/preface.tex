\vspace{-2cm}
This paper describes the report of the group project "DC-DC converter for PV module integration." It has been developed from the 10th of September to the 19th of December of 2018, at Aalborg University, Institute of Energy, by the group INTRO-760.

This document discusses the procedure and results of a DC to DC converter design to maximize the energy obtained from photovoltaic generation.

The literature references are shown in square brackets, with a number referring to a specific
document which can be found in the bibliography. If the reference is after the dot, it means that it refers to the whole previous paragraph. Pictures and tables will be denoted in
the X,Y format, with X representing the chapter and Y the figure or table number.
The process and development has been based on the Problem Based Learning (PBL) method.
